Company Culture and Influence on Innovation

Facebook's culture is characterized by its famous motto "Move Fast and Break Things." This approach encouraged rapid innovation, but also led to various challenges. The company values open communication, and its open floor plans at its headquarters are a testament to this. Facebook's culture has evolved over the years, with a greater emphasis on responsibility and building community.
