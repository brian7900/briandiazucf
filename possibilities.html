The Power of Computer Science

Computer science is not just about coding...

At its core, Computer Science is all problem-solving! We try to understand the world in a new way. Every challenge faced is an opportunity for innovation. From healthcare to entertainment, environment to space exploration, Computer Science plays a key role in driving advancements that touch every part of our lives.

Platforms to get started with

These platforms offer a glimpse into the world of Computer Science, providing tools, resources, and communities to help us start our journey!

CodeHS

CodeHS provides interactive and fun lessons in coding that offer courses ranging from JavaScript to Java. It's a place where you can build real-world projects and gain practical skills.

Scratch

Developed by MIT, Scratch is a visual programming language that makes it easy to create interactive stories, animations, and games. It's a vibrant community where you can share your creations and learn from others.

W3Schools

W3Schools offers tutorials and references on web development languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more. It's the perfect place for those whoa are new or veterans of web development. It also provides hands-on examples and exercises.

Code.org

Code.org is dedicated to expanding access to Computer Science in schools. With engaging curriculum and activities, it teaches foundational concepts of Computing.
